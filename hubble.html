What is Hubble?


Hubble is a website designed to help small content creators find other creators to collaborate with. You can make an account which is linked to your channel, and browse creators, filtering your search to find people of a similar video type, similar channel size or specific skillset. Collaboration requests will be directed your account, which you can accept or decline.

Why use it?

Hubble is easy to use and can connect you with people in the community that would be otherwise hard to find. How many times have you spent hours hunting for a collab partner on discord, only to find that they aren't serious enough, haven't established a channel, have poor quality content? Hubble can stop you from wasting time and allow you to actually focus on your content creation.
