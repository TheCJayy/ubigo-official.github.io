A Brief Explanation of Our Roles


Founder - Owner of ubigo, founded the company in 2021
CMO - Chief Marketing Officer, in charge of all marketing related aspects of ubigo such as advertising.
CFO - Chief Financial Officer, in charge of all of ubigo's finaces
CIO - Chief Information Officer, in charge of the technology and development of our projects
CTO - Chief Technology Officer, in charge of technology and technology related problems
Head of Customer Service - In charge of all customer service and customer help
Graphic Designer - Creates graphical content for the company such as logos, animations, banners, posters and more


We remain a small company at this stage which is why we have multiple roles, as we hopefully grow and develop, these positions will be given to other people who are right for the job and each person will have their one position. At this stage however it is important that we assign each team member multiple jobs to keep the company running smoothly.
